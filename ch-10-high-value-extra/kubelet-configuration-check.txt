======
Confirm kubelet settings

## FROM MANAGER
A- 
 - terminal one - kubectl proxy
 - terminal two - curl -X GET http://127.0.0.1:8001/api/v1/nodes/<node-name>/proxy/configz | jq .

B-
cat /var/lib/kubelet/config.yaml

--serialize-image-pulls=false 
to - /var/lib/kubelet/kubeadm-flag.env
